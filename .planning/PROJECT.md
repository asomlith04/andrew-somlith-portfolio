# FAUNA LUXE

## What This Is

FAUNA LUXE is a Shopify-based ecommerce brand selling luxury, aesthetic animal print MacBook cases. The brand targets fashion-forward students who want their laptop to be part of their personal aesthetic — positioned at $50–80, justified entirely by brand story and visual identity rather than mass-market price competition.

## Core Value

The brand makes owning a MacBook case feel like owning a fashion accessory — the FAUNA LUXE identity (cream & beige quiet luxury meets wild) is what separates it from a $12 Amazon case.

## Context

- Platform: Shopify (configured & themed, not headless/custom frontend)
- Fulfillment: Dropshipping — no inventory held, supplier ships directly
- Price tier: $50–80 aspirational luxury — brand positioning carries the premium
- Visual direction: Cream & beige quiet luxury — clean neutrals, minimal, very Pinterest/aesthetic
- Animal prints in v1: Leopard/cheetah, snake/python, cow print (3 hero prints)
- MacBook models: M2/M3 13" MacBook Air/Pro and M2/M3 14"/16" MacBook Pro (covers ~80% of buyers)
- Marketing channels: TikTok organic content, Instagram/Pinterest curation, influencer seeding, paid Meta/TikTok ads
- Target customer: Fashion-forward students, college-aged, trend-aware, laptop as aesthetic object

## Constraints

- **Platform**: Shopify — all store functionality built within Shopify ecosystem (themes, apps, Liquid)
- **Fulfillment**: Dropshipping supplier must be integrated before launch — order flow is critical path
- **Launch standard**: No soft launch — store must be functional AND beautiful on day one
- **Budget sensitivity**: Student market means perceived value must be exceptionally high to justify $50–80 price point
