**This is a creative production task.** The executor must produce 9 images that sell the luxury positioning. Photography is what separates $65 from $12.

**Step 1: Source base product photos**

Find supplier/stock images of animal print MacBook cases for each print (leopard, python/snake, cow/holstein). Sources to check:
- Supplier product pages (if the dropshipping supplier from Phase 4 research is already identified — check STATE.md or supplier catalogs like AliExpress, Zendrop, CJ Dropshipping)
- Stock photo sites for similar products (Unsplash, Pexels — search "animal print laptop case", "leopard MacBook case")
- If no real product photos are available yet: use high-quality stock images of animal print cases as temporary placeholders, clearly labeled as placeholders to be replaced in Phase 4 when supplier is confirmed

**CRITICAL (from research pitfall #4):** Do NOT use Midjourney or text-to-image AI to generate the case itself. AI-generated product images will NOT match the real product — wrong texture, wrong pattern density, wrong edge profile. Instead, use tools like Flair.ai that accept an UPLOADED photo of the real/representative product and replace ONLY the background, preserving the actual product texture.

**Step 2: Produce 3 image types per product using Flair.ai (or equivalent)**

For each of the 3 prints, produce:

1. **Hero shot** (becomes product thumbnail + collection card):
   - Upload base product photo to Flair.ai
   - Background: lifestyle desk scene — cream/beige minimalist
   - Prompt guidance: "editorial luxury product photo, MacBook case on minimalist cream desk, soft studio lighting, fashion editorial aesthetic, warm beige tones"
   - Case should be ON or next to a MacBook for scale context

2. **Flat lay**:
   - Upload base product photo
   - Background: cream linen or marble texture
   - Prompt guidance: "flat lay product photo, cream linen background, soft natural lighting, luxury fashion accessory"
   - Bird's-eye perspective, case centered

3. **Texture detail**:
   - Crop into the print pattern from the base photo
   - Background: cream/beige gradient or soft bokeh
   - Prompt guidance: "macro product photography, animal print texture detail, cream background, shallow depth of field"
   - Shows the quality of the print up close

**Step 3: Export and save**

- Export all images at maximum resolution from Flair.ai
- Resize to 2048x2048px square (Shopify recommended product image size)
- Save as JPEG with high quality (85%+)
- Save to: `.planning/phases/02-product-catalog/photography/` using naming convention:
  - `{print}-hero.jpg`, `{print}-flatlay.jpg`, `{print}-texture.jpg`
  - Where {print} = leopard, python, holstein

**If Flair.ai is not accessible or free tier is exhausted:**
- Alternative 1: PhotoRoom (similar product-specific AI background replacement)
- Alternative 2: Canva with background remover + cream/beige background templates
- Alternative 3: Manual photo editing (remove background, composite onto cream backdrop)
- Last resort: Use high-quality stock photos with cream/beige aesthetic as TEMPORARY placeholders, document as such in SUMMARY, flag for Phase 3 or 4 replacement

**Discretion items (Claude decides):**
- Exact hero shot backdrop style (minimalist cream vs lifestyle desk vs editorial — all acceptable within cream/beige locked constraint)
- Collection page thumbnail strategy: reuse hero image for collection cards (simplest, recommended)
